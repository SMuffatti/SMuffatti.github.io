About

Oh hey there, my name is Stephen and I am a web developer - but I assume you knew that already. I am self taught and constantly seeking to learn more as I create and build things for the web. I may not be the best or the smartest developer, but I am persistent and I firmly believe in doing thengs the right way.

I try and live life as free as possible and to constantly seek happiness and productivity. In everything that I do, I try and apply the simple life rules of adapt, overcome, and improvise.
