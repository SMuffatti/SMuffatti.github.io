About

Oh hey there, my name is Stephen and I am a web developer. I am self taught and constantly seeking to learn more as I create and build things for the web. I may not be the best or the brightest developer, but I am one of the most persistent and I firmly believe in doing things the right way.


Apart from web development, I maintain several hobbies - all of which I am constantly expanding and improving on. I enjoy growing plants from vegetables to exotic cacti and succulents. I make my own wine and my own beer in a homemade electric brewery. I mill my own lumber and handcraft woodworking projects. When I want to relax, there's nothing better than some Dungeons & Dragons or a boardgame. I have an ever increasing number of animals in my life and the most disinteresting trait I have, is my love for drinking gin in the evening while reading a rule book or tax code.


I try and live life as freely as possible and to constantly seek happiness and productivity. In everything that I do, I try and apply the simple life rules of: adapt, overcome, and improvise.
